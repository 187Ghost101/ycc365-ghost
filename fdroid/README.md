## 🎯 What F-Droid Is

- 100% open-source app store
- No tracking, no ads, no Google Play Services
- Apps signed with F-Droid keys (NOT vendor keys)
- ~2M+ monthly downloads
- Strict quality requirements
- Acts as an alternative distribution for security/privacy-aware users
